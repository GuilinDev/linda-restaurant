Our Story

Welcome to Red Dragon, where authentic Chinese cuisine meets exceptional taste, right in the heart of Scottdale. As the premier destination for Chinese dining in the area, we take pride in offering an unparalleled culinary experience that tantalizes taste buds and transports guests to the vibrant streets of China.

We genuinely love our customers, we are blessed to have the best customers in all of Phoenix, we want to help those in need and make a positive difference in Phoenix through every dish, dessert, and drink we offer.

We believe that the true essence of Chinese cuisine lies in the perfect balance of flavors, textures, and aromas. Our talented chefs, steeped in tradition and innovation, meticulously craft each dish with the freshest ingredients and time-honored techniques, ensuring every bite is a celebration of culinary mastery.

Whether it's a casual dinner with loved ones or a special celebration with friends, we invite you to savor the flavors of China with us and experience why we're renowned as the best Chinese restaurant in the area.
